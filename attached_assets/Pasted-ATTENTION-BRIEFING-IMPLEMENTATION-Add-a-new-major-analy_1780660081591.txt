ATTENTION BRIEFING IMPLEMENTATION

Add a new major analysis section called:

ATTENTION BRIEFING

This section should be treated exactly like the existing Security Briefing and Structure Briefing systems.

It must have:

- Its own checklist scoring system
- Its own Tier grading system
- Its own Verdict
- Detailed numbered breakdown
- Contribution to the final overall recommendation

Do NOT make this a simple social sentiment module.

The goal is to measure whether a token is gaining attention faster than the market can price it in.

The system should focus on identifying attention ignition, narrative strength, and early-stage momentum.

==================================================
ATTENTION TIER SYSTEM

Create a checklist scoring system.

Example:

Attention Tier: A
Score: 26/30 (87%)
Verdict: HIGH ATTENTION POTENTIAL

Suggested grading:

A = Exceptional Attention Potential
B = Strong Attention Potential
C = Moderate Attention Potential
D = Weak Attention Potential
F = Attention Deficit / Avoid

This should mirror the Security and Structure grading systems.

==================================================
CORE SCORING PILLARS

Only score the highest-value signals.

---

1. NARRATIVE STRENGTH (0-10)

---

Evaluate:

- One-line explainability
- Emotional intensity
- Familiarity
- Meme strength
- Cultural relevance

Questions:

Can the token be explained instantly?

Does it trigger:

- humor
- outrage
- absurdity
- FOMO
- tribalism

Is the narrative naturally shareable?

Examples of strong narratives:

- animals
- politics
- celebrities
- AI
- viral clips
- scandals
- trending events

Output:

Narrative Strength: X/10

---

2. ATTENTION VELOCITY (0-10)

---

This is one of the highest-weighted signals.

Measure:

- Mention acceleration
- Mention growth rate
- Unique poster growth rate
- Engagement acceleration

Focus on acceleration rather than total volume.

Example:

5 → 10 → 20 → 40 mentions

is stronger than

100 → 100 → 100 → 100 mentions

Output:

Attention Velocity: X/10

---

3. DISTRIBUTION STRENGTH (0-5)

---

Measure:

- Unique accounts posting
- Community spread
- Independent promotion
- Influencer/KOL presence

The goal is determining whether attention is spreading naturally.

Example:

50 posts from 40 unique accounts

is stronger than

50 posts from 5 accounts

Output:

Distribution Strength: X/5

---

4. NARRATIVE FRESHNESS (0-5)

---

Measure:

- Narrative originality
- Meme saturation
- Clone detection
- Recycled concepts

Questions:

Has this narrative recently run?

Is this merely another variation of an exhausted meme?

Fresh narratives should score higher.

Output:

Freshness Score: X/5

==================================================
ATTENTION LIFECYCLE 
==================================================

This is a universal Attention Lifecycle system that works for:

- Pre-bond tokens
- Newly bonded tokens
- Established runners
- Mid-cap memes
- Large-cap memes
- Revival plays
- Old coins regaining attention

Classifications:

1. EMERGING

Characteristics:
- Attention beginning to appear
- Mentions increasing
- Awareness still low
- Early discovery phase

Interpretation:
Bullish early-stage attention.

--------------------------------------------------

2. ACCELERATING

Characteristics:
- Attention growing rapidly
- Mention velocity increasing
- More unique participants

Interpretation:
Strong momentum phase.

--------------------------------------------------

3. DOMINANT

Characteristics:
- Attention leader within its narrative
- Broad discussion
- High visibility

Interpretation:
Strong but higher-risk phase.

--------------------------------------------------

4. EXHAUSTING

Characteristics:
- Attention plateauing
- Growth slowing
- Engagement weakening

Interpretation:
Momentum fading.

--------------------------------------------------

5. DORMANT

Characteristics:
- Very low attention
- Narrative inactive
- Limited discussion

Interpretation:
Not necessarily bearish.
Potential future revival candidate.

--------------------------------------------------

6. REVIVING

Characteristics:
- Dormant token regaining attention
- New social activity
- Narrative returning

Interpretation:
Potential high-upside reactivation.

==================================================
ATTENTION EFFICIENCY SCORE
==================================================

ATTENTION EFFICIENCY

Purpose:

Measure how effectively attention converts into market cap appreciation.

Formula Concept:

Market Cap Growth
÷
Attention Growth

Examples:

HIGH:

Moderate attention increase
Large market cap increase

MEDIUM:

Attention and price growing similarly

LOW:

Huge attention increase
Weak market cap response

Output:

Attention Efficiency:
- HIGH
- MEDIUM
- LOW

Display explanation.

Example:

"Attention is converting into buyers efficiently."

or

"High attention but weak market response."

IMPORTANT DESIGN PRINCIPLE

The purpose of the Attention Briefing is NOT to predict winners.

The purpose is to measure whether attention is accelerating faster than the market is pricing it in.

================================================
FINAL DESIGN PRINCIPLE
==================================================

The highest-conviction entries occur when:

1. Security is strong.
2. Structure is strong.
3. Attention is accelerating.
4. Holder growth confirms attention.
5. Ownership is broadening.
6. Attention converts efficiently into price appreciation.

The system should identify situations where:

Strong Security
+
Strong Structure
+
Strong Attention
+
Strong Holder Growth

align simultaneously.

These represent the highest-quality trading opportunities.
