
HOLDER ANALYSIS REFINEMENTS
======================
SEPARATE HOLDER COUNT QUALITY FROM HOLDER MOMENTUM
========================================================

Problem:

Current Holder Momentum is based primarily on current holder count versus a threshold.

This measures adoption size.

It does NOT measure momentum.

========================================================
NEW HOLDER COUNT QUALITY
========================================================

Purpose:

Measure current adoption size.

Inputs:

Current Holder Count

Minimum Holder Threshold

Output:

Holder Count Quality

Range:

0–100

Classifications:

Excellent

Strong

Adequate

Weak

Insufficient

Question Answered:

"Does the token have enough holders?"

========================================================
NEW HOLDER MOMENTUM
========================================================

Purpose:

Measure holder growth or decline over time.

Preferred Data Sources:

1. Helius

2. Solscan

3. Any available holder-history endpoint

========================================================
TRACK
========================================================

Current Holders

1 Hour Ago Holders

6 Hours Ago Holders

24 Hours Ago Holders

========================================================
CALCULATE
========================================================

1H Holder Change %

6H Holder Change %

24H Holder Change %

Weighted Formula:

HolderMomentumScore =

(1H Change × 0.50)

+

(6H Change × 0.30)

+

(24H Change × 0.20)

Normalize to:

0–100

========================================================

========================================================

75–89

60–74

45–59

30–44

0–29

Holder Exodus

========================================================
ADD HOLDER DIVERGENCE
========================================================

Purpose:

Detect hidden accumulation or distribution.

========================================================
ACCUMULATION DIVERGENCE
========================================================

Condition:

Price Falling

AND

Holder Count Rising

Display:

Accumulation Divergence

Meaning:

New holders continue entering despite price weakness.

Bullish signal.

========================================================
DISTRIBUTION DIVERGENCE
========================================================

Condition:

Price Rising

AND

Holder Count Falling

Display:

Distribution Divergence

Meaning:

Participation is shrinking despite price appreciation.

Bearish signal.

========================================================

ENTRY CHECKLIST INTEGRATION
========================================================

Examples:

✓ Strong Holder Growth

⚠ Stable Holder Growth

✗ Holder Exodus

✗ Distribution Divergence

========================================================
DATA AVAILABILITY RULE
========================================================

If holder history cannot be obtained:

Display:

"Historical holder data unavailable."

Show:

Holder Count Quality only.

Do not estimate or fabricate Holder Momentum.

Reduce Holder Momentum confidence accordingly.
