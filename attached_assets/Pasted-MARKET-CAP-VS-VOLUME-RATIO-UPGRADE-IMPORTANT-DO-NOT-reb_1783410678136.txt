MARKET CAP VS VOLUME RATIO UPGRADE

IMPORTANT

DO NOT rebuild the Security Engine.

DO NOT remove or modify any existing Security Checks, Rug Checks, Holder Analysis, Bundle Detection, Developer Analysis or Security Scoring.

The goal is to add an additional validation metric based on the relationship between Market Cap and 24H Trading Volume.

---

OBJECTIVE

Calculate the Volume-to-Market Cap Ratio to identify coins with suspiciously low trading activity relative to their Market Cap.

This metric should act as an early warning indicator for potential bundled, manipulated or unhealthy trading conditions.

---

FORMULA

Calculate:

Volume to Market Cap Ratio (%) = (24H Volume USD ÷ Market Cap USD) × 100

Example:

Market Cap = $500,000

24H Volume = $600,000

Volume Ratio

= (600,000 ÷ 500,000) × 100

= 120%

---

CLASSIFICATION

Display both the calculated percentage and a qualitative rating.

Suggested thresholds:

≥ 150%
Excellent

120% – 149%
Very Healthy

100% – 119%
Healthy

80% – 99%
Borderline

Below 80%
High Risk

Below 50%
Critical Risk

---

SECURITY ANALYSIS

Provide a brief interpretation based on the ratio.

Examples:

If Ratio ≥ 100%

"Trading volume exceeds Market Cap, indicating healthy market participation and liquidity."

If Ratio is between 80% and 99%

"Trading volume is approaching the minimum acceptable threshold. Exercise additional caution."

If Ratio < 80%

"Trading volume is below the recommended minimum relative to Market Cap. This may indicate bundled wallets, weak liquidity, artificial valuation, or reduced market participation. Treat this as a significant security warning."
