IMPROVE:

Bundle Risk Evaluation in the Cabal/Security Briefing & Checklist 

REPLACE BUNDLE FAIL WITH BUNDLE RISK
========================================================

Problem:

Current logic treats bundle presence as a major fail condition.

Modern Solana memecoins frequently launch with bundled participation.

The existence of bundles alone should not automatically imply manipulation or invalidate a trade.

========================================================
NEW BUNDLE RISK SYSTEM
========================================================

Bundle FAIL

Replace with:

Range:

0–100

========================================================

========================
GUIDING PRINCIPLE
========================================================

Bundle presence alone is not a red flag.

Bundle quality and concentration determine risk.

Holder count measures adoption size.

Holder momentum measures adoption growth.

These are separate metrics and should remain separate throughout the system.

NOTE: In the Security Checklist, rename "Bundle Risk" to "Bundle Concentration Risk". That makes it immediately clear that I'm evaluating how the bundles are distributed, not simply punishing a token for having bundles.
